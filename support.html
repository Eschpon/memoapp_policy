Simple Calendar Memo

About the App

myplan is a simple iOS app that lets you add memos to dates on a calendar.

  • Select a date and write, save, or delete memos
  • Categorize dates with 3 color markers (orange, green, purple)
  • Search memos by keyword and date range
  • Japanese national holidays displayed automatically (2020â€“2050)
  • Dark mode support
  • Japanese / English language support

FAQ

Q. Where is my data stored?
A. All data is stored locally on your device. It is never sent to external servers.
Q. Do I need to create an account?
A. No. No account registration or internet connection is required.
Q. Can I back up my data?
A. Yes. Your data is included in iCloud or iTunes backups.
Q. Can I transfer data to a new device?
A. Yes. Data is transferred through iPhone migration (Quick Start) or iCloud backup restore.
Q. What years are holidays supported?
A. Japanese national holidays from 2020 to 2050 are supported, including substitute holidays.
